Venom: the guardian of death is a science fiction film jointly produced by Columbia Pictures, Tencent pictures and marvel pictures, and released by Sony Pictures Entertainment. It is directed by Ruben Fleischer and starred by Tom Hardy, Michelle Williams and Liz Ahmed. Adapted from Marvel comics, the film tells the story of Eddie Bullock who is invaded and controlled by an unknown alien symbiont and becomes an alternative superhero.
